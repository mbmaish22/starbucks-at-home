Caramel Macchiato

Rich espresso, steamed milk, and vanilla syrup topped with caramel sauce

Caramel Macchiato Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots espresso
  • 1 cup steamed milk
  • 2 tbsp vanilla syrup
  • 2 tbsp caramel sauce
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add vanilla syrup to mug
  2. Pour steamed milk
  3. Add espresso shots on top
  4. Drizzle with caramel sauce
  5. Add whipped cream if desired

Caramel Frappuccino

Blended iced coffee drink with caramel flavor and whipped cream

Caramel Frappuccino Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strong coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp caramel sauce
  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Blend coffee, milk, 2 tbsp caramel, ice, and sugar
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy
  3. Pour into glass
  4. Top with whipped cream
  5. Drizzle remaining caramel on top

Vanilla Latte

Smooth espresso with steamed milk and sweet vanilla syrup

Vanilla Latte Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots espresso
  • 1 cup steamed milk
  • 2 tbsp vanilla syrup
  • Milk foam
  • Cinnamon (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add vanilla syrup to mug
  2. Pour in espresso shots
  3. Add steamed milk
  4. Top with milk foam
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon if desired
